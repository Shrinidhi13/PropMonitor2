Vision:

To build the Google of Real Estate industry.

About Us:

PropMonitor is an online property transaction tracker tool. We are a PropTech startup based in India that aims to bring transparency to the real estate industry by making transaction data available to the public. We believe that access to reliable and up-to-date information can empower buyers, sellers, investors, and agents to make informed decisions and negotiate better deals.

Our platform collects, analyzes, and displays data from various sources, such as government records, property portals, brokers, and surveys. We provide insights on market trends, price movements, demand and supply, inventory, and more. Whether you are looking for a new home, selling your property, investing in real estate, or seeking professional advice, PropMonitor can help you with data-driven solutions.

What is our goal?

Our goal is to create the world's most extensive database of property transactions. In India, where real estate is complex and data is limited, we aim to provide the public with the information they need to make informed investment decisions. We are committed to bridging the gap in the real estate market and providing valuable insights for our users.

How can you Help?

The general public can contribute to building our database by providing information about property transactions in their area. This could include details such as sale prices, property features, and transaction dates. Additionally, individuals can share their knowledge of local real estate trends and market conditions. By collaborating with the public, we can gather a wealth of valuable data and insights that will enrich our database and benefit the community as a whole.

What are the benefits for Real Estate Professionals?

Real Estate professionals, including brokers, property valuers/appraisers, engineers, and others, have the opportunity to collaborate and contribute their data to create a free and open-source database. In return, they will gain access to a valuable source of potential leads. The more they contribute, the more features they can unlock, enhancing their access to valuable information and opportunities within the industry.
